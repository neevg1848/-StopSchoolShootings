About: Mission and Vision

Mission and Vision

The Mission: To educate the public about gun violence, and its impact on school students. By working with institutions to pass laws and practices that improve the safety of school children, we want to reduce the average number of school shootings.

The Vision: To empower school children to learn, develop, and flourish in a safe atmosphere.


Core Values

Security: We believe every student deserves a sheltered environment to reach their full potential

Harmony: We envision a society in which students serve as supportive pillars for one another and synthesize generational peace.

Empower: We aspire to facilitate students with the appropriate conditions to enable them to expedite a preferable future.
