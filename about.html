So far, we have implemented and plan to take a variety of measures to avoid school shootings, including:

Podcast: What Now?

Our weekly podcast informs our listeners in brief episodes on the prevalence and magnitude of school shootings. Furthermore, the podcast suggests several suitable methods for the listener to implement into their daily lives that enhance the safety of the public one step at a time.

Social Media: Instagram:
@Help.stopschoolschootings

Follow @Help.stopschoolshootings to stay up to date on the latest school shooting news. On our official account, we post pertinent information, future plans, and methods to contribute to a brighter future!

PSA: More Gun Control Laws?

This PSA describes why guncontrol laws are not only feasible but also quintessential change necessary to reduce school shootings. The PSA outlines an example of rudimentary gun control measures that would lessen the injuries caused by mass shootings (like school shootings) while having a negligible impact on the ordinary gun owner.

Article: School Shootings in America

This article provides an overview of several aspects of school shootings, including shooters and survivors. The report provides the reader with a sturdy informational foundation to grasp supplemental resources covering further scientific and societal aspects of school shootings.

Coming-Soon: Official Proposals

We are creating several proposals for various institutions (like schools, governments, and police stations) around the United States, which propose effective legislation and reforms that will indirectly and directly help prevent school shootings.

Coming-Soon: Protests

Although not officially sanctioned, we intend to organize several protests in major cities and places around the United States. These rallies aim to advocate for various strategies to decrease school shootings and pressure the pertinent institutions to reform and change.
